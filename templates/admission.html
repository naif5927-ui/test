ADMISSION

Admission is generally offered to anyone, without consideration of caste,
creed, religion or sex, as per rules and regulations of the school and as long
as there are vacancies. However the management reserves the right to deny
admission to anyone without assigning any reason whatsoever.
(a) Application for admission must be made by the parent or legal guardian
in the prescribed from in the month of March or April during the previous
academic year.
(b) Only children who have completed three years on 1st of January of the
year of admission will be admitted to Lower K.G. class and same rule will be
followed in admission to Higher classes.
(c) Pupils who intend to join the AIEM from other schools or after private
study will have to appear for an entrance examination. Those who come from
other schools should also produce transfer certificates. Those who join the
nursery classes should produce birth certificates and Adhar.
(d) In the case of new admissions, fees for all previous months of the year of
admission will have to be paid irrespective of the term, month or date of
admission.
(e) All pupils in the primary and pre-primary classes are expected to be
vaccinated against small pox and to have undergone a full course of triple
antigen and polio vaccine before joining the school.
(f) Transfer Certificate will ordinarily be issued only at the end of an
academic year.
(g) Application for the Transfer Certificate should be made in writing to the
Principal by the parent before the end of April.
(h) Before granting a transfer Certificate at any other time the fee for the
whole term should be paid.
